Welcome to Tar Heel Music!

Play real-time notes from four different instruments (drum, guitar, bass, piano) and continue your recorded melody based off Machine Learning. Learn how to play below!

Play Notes
  • Press or click letters s, d, x, c to play notes.
  • Press z to shuffle the note pitches.
  • Press 'a' to switch instruments.
Record Your Melody
  • Click or press 'r' to start recording. To stop recording, click the button again.
Main Features
  • saves your original note sequence to your device as a MIDI file.
  • continues your recorded note sequence using Machine Learning.
  • gives the accessible option of playing notes with two larger buttons.
  • The button plays your original recorded sequence.
